LTEC 5200

Coding to Learn

A collection of news, projects, and resources that can be utilized by instructors or parents to teach a variety of concepts through programming.

This site will cover success stories from educators and other related articles, while providing a wealth of resources and example projects that instructors can begin implementing in their own learning environments. The collection of content won't focus on a particular programming language or platform, but instead will attempt to demonstrate a variety of methods and products that can be used to introduce skills universal to problem solving. As more and more devices become digital, it is important to provide a basis for understanding how to control the items, services, conveniences afforded to us via modern technology.
